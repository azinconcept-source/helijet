]]> There’s just a few days until Rock The Shores Festival 2016 hits the stage in Colwood, BC.  The West Shore Parks & Recreation fields will be alive with the sounds of Current Swell, City and Colour, Metric, Arkells, Cat Empire, Jon & Roy, Yukon Blonde and more.

While the festival site is easily accessible via car, transit, bike and foot what better way could there be than to arrive by air. Get the true Rock Star experience and fly into the site, just a few short minutes from the Downtown Victoria Heliport.
Helijet can get you there, board your flight at the heliport and in just minutes you’ll be making your big entrance as the helicopter lands beside the stage.  Staff will escort you into the site for your ultimate Rock The Shore arrival…one that is definitely worth Snapchatting about.

Flights depart from Victoria Harbour Heliport, 79 Dallas Road at the following times;

Friday July 22nd – 2:10pm & 6:40pm
Saturday July 23rd – 12:50pm, 1:30pm & 4:15pm
Sunday July 24th – 12:50pm & 1:30pm

One-Way Fare from Victoria Harbour Heliport to Rock The Shores Festival site is $85 including tax.
Please note: flights are one-way, TO Rock The Shores, guests must make their own transportation arrangements to depart from Rock The Shores after the shows.

Call Helijet Reservation at 1.800.665.4354 to book your flight today!
