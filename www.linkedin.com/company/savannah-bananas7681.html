About us

The Savannah Bananas are a collegiate summer league baseball team located in Savannah, GA. Known for crazy antics and non-stop entertainment, the Bananas strive to create the best Fan Experience in the world. The mission of "Fans First. Entertain Always." guides the Bananas in everything they do. The team is owned by Fans First Entertainment and started operations in Savannah in the fall of 2015. When the team name launched in February 2016 the story went viral with coverage from ESPN, CBS, Yahoo and in other news outlets throughout the country. As a result, Savannah Bananas merchandise has been sold in all 50 states and internationally. Welcoming over 100,000 fans each summer to Grayson Stadium, the Bananas sell-out every home game as fans from all over the world come to Savannah to see dancing players, coaches, and the senior-citizen dance team, the Banana Nanas. The Bananas believing in always being Caring, Different, Enthusiastic, Fun, Growing, and Hungry. That's the Fans First Way.
