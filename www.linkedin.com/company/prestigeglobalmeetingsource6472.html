About us

40 Years of Elevating Events | It’s not just what we do, but how we do it, that makes us stand out in the marketplace. Since 1986, Prestige Global Meeting Source has grown into one of the most trusted names in site selection and event services. Through industry shifts and challenges, our focus has remained constant: we help planners at every level deliver events that shine. Our deep industry relationships, trusted reputation, and collaborative approach allow us to deliver better options, faster answers, and greater peace of mind.
