About us

Virtuoso is the leading global network of travel agencies specializing in luxury and experiential travel, with more than 20,000 travel advisors. We partner with over 1,800 of the world’s best companies such as hotels, cruise lines, tour operators, and more. Our advisors use their personal connections and firsthand expertise to craft bespoke trips for clients, including unique experiences, special values, complimentary perks, VIP treatment and rare access. For more information on becoming a Virtuoso travel advisor or preferred partner, please visit virtuoso.com/why-virtuoso/Join-Virtuoso.

    What’s shaping the future of luxury travel? At the 2026 Virtuoso Symposium in Seoul, over 350 leaders from 33 countries came together to explore exactly that. The insights are clear: • Performance within the Virtuoso network is outpacing global trends, growing at roughly twice the rate of the wider travel market • Sales by preferred partners rose 20% year-over-year at the start of 2026, with continued double-digit growth • Bookings made 1–2 years in advance are up 23%, signalling strong long-term demand • 78% of Virtuoso agency leaders expect sales to increase this year, with many forecasting significant growth At the same time, traveler behavior continues to evolve: • 72% of advisors say clients are prioritising discovering new destinations • Nearly half point to a rise in slower, more immersive travel experiences Despite global uncertainty, luxury travel remains resilient, driven by a desire for deeper, more meaningful experiences. #VirtuosoEvent
