About us

Amex GBT Meetings & Events is a trusted global agency with more than 40 years of experience, presence in over 140 countries, and a team of 1,800+ professionals worldwide. Each year, we deliver more than 67,000 events, from conferences and corporate incentives to exclusive brand activations, that help organizations connect, inspire, and achieve business results. We create unforgettable experiences that leave a lasting impact on attendees, companies, and brands. Through our global marketplace, decades of expertise, and innovative technology, we offer fully customizable services tailored to specific goals, whether for a single event or as part of a managed meetings program. Our teams bring together unmatched talent, creativity, technology, and scale to design memorable experiences that are also more inclusive and sustainable. As a trusted partner worldwide, from ideation to execution, we bring clients' visions to life and deliver measurable, impactful results.
