About us

With more than 75 years of aviation excellence across the globe, Bristow is the global leader in innovative and sustainable vertical lift transportation, search and rescue (SAR), and aircraft support solutions to energy customers and government and civil organizations. Bristow is a proven leader in safety and training standards and is equipped with a diverse fleet of the industry’s most modern aircraft to support the growing demand for safe and reliable aviation solutions. Bristow currently has customers in Australia, Brazil, Canada, Chile, the Dutch Caribbean, the Falkland Islands, Ireland, the Netherlands, Nigeria, Norway, Spain, Suriname, Trinidad, the United Kingdom (UK) and the United States (US).
