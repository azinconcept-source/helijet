About us

Fora is the modern travel agency, powering a new generation of travel entrepreneurs. Our AI-enabled platform provides advisors with the technology, training, and community to build real businesses and deliver trips no algorithm alone could match. More than 15,000 travel advisors have built businesses on Fora’s platform, 97% of whom are new to booking travel. Together, they have booked more than $2.5 billion in travel across 180+ countries.     AI is not going to replace the travel advisor (but I do get asked this all. the. time!). It's going to make Fora Travel Advisors more defensible than ever. Glenn Fogel, Booking.com CEO (not exactly an advisor cheerleader), recently said technology will recreate the human travel agent "only much, much better." He's half right. Here's the structural reason: there are three layers to planning a trip. AI will get the first one. The other two are why advisors get more defensible from here, not less. 1) AI knows my clients. I recently asked Claude to recommend a hotel in Paris for my clients based on their previous trips. It recommended the exact same options I had in mind.                                                                        But knowing you isn't the whole job. 2) We need taste and a network. AI can read TripAdvisor and Condé Nast. It can't read our Fora X group chat (100s of messages a day) or text the GM to get you a room upgrade. 3) We get the offline stuff right. AI doesn't know the best tour guide in Rome (his name is Luca, DM me for an intro 😏), so you end up on a generic GetYourGuide tour that AI could scrape. Experiences, like tours, DMCs, and multi-day tours, are now a $271B market and the fastest-growing segment in travel (Phocuswright/Arival, 2025). Many of the highest quality experiences are still very much offline. The best version of this isn't AI vs. advisors. It's AI + advisors, who use AI to handle research and comparison so they can spend more of their time on the work AI can't touch - leveraging their network to plan the best trip for you.
