About us

Immerse yourself in an authentic Hawaiian experience deeply rooted in local culture, environmental adventure and a warm Aloha spirit at the newly transformed Ritz-Carlton Maui, Kapalua. Breathtaking oceanfront, natural beauty inspires all senses as redesigned guest rooms and suites bring the colors and textures of Hawaii's lush tropical landscape inside to create a natural and restorative sanctuary. An enhanced lobby and pool experience and new dining venues feature a fresh design approach reflective of Kapalua’s lush surroundings while award-winning culinary experiences and a Hawaiian-inspired spa culminate an unforgettable escape.
