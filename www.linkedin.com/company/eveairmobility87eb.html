About us

Eve is developing a full portfolio of solutions to enable the UAM market and ultimately benefit people’s lives, including the progression and certification of the company’s electric vertical takeoff and landing vehicle (eVTOL), the associated comprehensive services and support network, and the creation of urban air traffic management solutions.

    At today’s Florida Department of Transportation AAM Symposium, held at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, our CCO Megha B. and industry peers emphasized the leadership, teamwork and long-term planning needed to turn advanced air mobility (AAM) from vision into reality. This year, Florida made real progress by creating a strong framework for AAM infrastructure investment. The state now has new authority to support public vertiport projects and accelerate the transition from planning to action. This is important because the next stage of AAM needs more than just technology. It calls for disciplined public investment, shared infrastructure, utility readiness, clear operational standards and a path to services that work in the real world. Eve is proud to be a long-time partner in Florida’s AAM community. We work closely with federal, state and local programs to help turn policy progress into real infrastructure and benefits for communities. Eve offers more than just an eVTOL aircraft. We provide OEM expertise, strong operator relationships, ecosystem planning, service and support capabilities, and digital infrastructure skills. All of this is backed by Embraer’s 56 years of aviation experience, supporting a new era in aviation. #AdvancedAirMobility #AAM #Aviation #TransportationInnovation #Florida #FDOT #PublicPrivatePartnerships #FutureOfMobility #Infrastructure

    Today, we reported our 1Q 2026 results, and what stands out most is the continued momentum across our development and certification journey. Our engineering prototype flight campaign is progressing strongly. Since the first flight in December, we have expanded the flight envelope, gained valuable knowledge and improved performance through real-world testing. We have now completed over 50 successful flights, a key milestone as we prepare for the next phases of the campaign. We were also proud to show our prototype to Brazilian authorities, including Brazil’s President Lula. This demonstration highlights not only our progress but also the strong support for advanced air mobility. We are also moving forward with certification, working closely with ANAC - Agência Nacional de Aviação Civil and global regulators to set the path toward type certification. At the same time, we are getting ready to assemble our certification prototypes, which will validate the aircraft for commercial use. Each of these milestones shows the strength of our approach. We combine Embraer’s proven methods with thorough testing, simulation and system integration to accelerate development while maintaining the highest safety standards. We are excited for what’s ahead as we keep working toward entry into service. Read the full report at ir.eveairmobility.com/ #EveAirMobility #MobilityReimagined #eVTOL
