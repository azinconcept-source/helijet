About us

Joby is a California-based company building quiet, all-electric aircraft to connect people like never before. Quiet as a conversation and with the ability to take off and land vertically, the Joby aircraft will change the way we move while reducing the acoustic and climate footprint of flight.

    I've had the privilege to work on more than 100 Flight Simulation Training Devices in my 20 year career in simulation, but this one is special. The level of realism is unmatched in a commercial training device. The pilot steps into the flight deck the same way they do in real life, through an actual aircraft door. Once they sit down at the real flight controls, they have an uninterrupted field of view of the outside world, including through the window of that same aircraft door. As they navigate stunning environments rendered by CAE’s Prodigy image generator, pilots will experience the nuance of dynamic wind flow around structures. Every sound and vibration is driven by Joby’s proprietary host simulation, the culmination of years of rigorous development by our engineers to ensure our simulators and the aircraft are completely in sync. I'm incredibly proud to have lead this project and I look forward to the years of high quality training that this device will provide.

    We’re hiring for multiple positions across our Flight Test Department at Joby 🚁 What makes flight test at Joby so unique is the range of work that we get to do on a day-to-day basis.
