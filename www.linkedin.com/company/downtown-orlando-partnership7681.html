About us

The Downtown Orlando Partnership (DOP) is a 501(c)(6) organization founded in 1961 that focuses specifically on Downtown Orlando's business community. The DOP offers more than 280 corporate members a single point of access to connect, collaborate, and contribute to the growth of our community. Our membership represents the diverse size and scope that makes Downtown Orlando a vibrant beacon of development in Central Florida. The DOP is governed by a board of directors and trustees, a group of business leaders who are passionate about supporting the downtown Orlando district. The Executive Committee includes a president, president-elect, and a treasurer. Board committees exist to help guide the activities of the DOP. MISSION The mission of the Downtown Orlando Partnership is to enhance business and community relationships through collaborative events and initiatives. VISION Downtown Orlando is an active community recognized as the premier destination of choice for business, community and culture. We envision Downtown Orlando as a thriving business community filled with prosperity, and as the premier destination for our community to live, work and play. STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES Arts & Culture Education Health & Wellness Lodging & Dining Quality of Life Real Estate & Development Sports & Entertainment Technology & Innovation
