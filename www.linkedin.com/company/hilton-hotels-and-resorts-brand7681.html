    Please join me in celebrating the unveiling of the reimagined Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus, marking a bold step forward for Istanbul’s most iconic hotel overlooking the Bosphorus. Hilton Istanbul Bosphorus needs no introduction, as it is truly one of the most iconic hotels in the world. Opened in 1955 by Conrad Hilton himself, it was Hilton's very first Hilton hotel opened outside of the Americas, and Europe's first modern built hotel post World War II. The hotel is known to have defined Turkish hospitality, has hosted countless celebrities, including Sophia Lauren, Louis Armstrong and Muhammed Ali, and has appeared in many famous movies, including the opening scene of James Bond's "Russia with Love". The transformation spans 475 guest rooms and suites, includes more than 4,000 square meters of event space - continuing the hotel’s longstanding history as a destination for celebrations, cultures and connections. Food and drink sit at the heart of the hotel’s reinvention, reestablishing its role as one of Istanbul’s most vibrant gathering places, where conversation, culture and flavor come together throughout the day.
