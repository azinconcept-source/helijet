About CHC Helicopter

We are a global leader in helicopter crew transportation and search and rescue services. We deliver safe and reliable services to the oil and gas and offshore wind industries, as well as serving as the frontline emergency response to local communities around the world. Our sister business, Heli-One, is also an industry-leading provider of helicopter maintenance, repair, and overhaul services. We are proud to employ some of the most seasoned technical experts in the world. If you're passionate about aviation, and interested in learning more about the work we do, visit our website.

Join a team making a real difference
