About us

Mission - The Incentive Research Foundation funds and promotes research to advance the science, enhance the awareness and improve the appropriate application of motivation and incentives in business globally. The goal is to increase the understanding, effective use and resultant benefits of incentives to businesses that currently use incentives and others interested in improved performance.
