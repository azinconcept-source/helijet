About us

HelmsBriscoe streamlines the meeting planning process by managing the time-consuming task of researching, contacting, and evaluating venues for your organization’s events. Since 1992, HelmsBriscoe clients have benefited from our global reach, unsurpassed experience, and industry relationships. Whether you are booking a large-scale association event, or a smaller corporate function, our team of industry professionals will work to match your meeting with the best venue at the best value based on your specific needs. For more information, please contact our global headquarters at info@helmsbriscoe.com or 480.718.1111

    Los Angeles was honored to host the 2026 HelmsBriscoe Annual Business Conference for the very first time, welcoming more than 1,000 global meetings and events professionals to experience our destination firsthand. Centered at the Los Angeles Convention Center, the conference created an extraordinary opportunity to showcase the energy and evolution of LA as a premier meetings and events destination. Through immersive regional tours across 50+ TMD hotels, activations with venue partners across all five regions and pre-event experiences for more than 200 meeting planners, attendees experienced the vibrance, accessibility and diversity that make Los Angeles unlike anywhere else, while gaining a firsthand look at the Convention Center’s exciting future as a transformed citywide anchor. Perhaps most meaningful was the feedback from attendees, many of whom had not visited Los Angeles in years and left with a renewed perspective on the destination and the momentum happening across our city. As Richard Harper, EVP of HelmsBriscoe, shared: “Los Angeles provided the perfect backdrop for our company’s conference… our attendees loved the vibrance of the city, the convenience and comfort of the host properties, and the overall experience afforded by the destination.” Thank you to our hotel, venue and city partners who helped bring this experience to life and continue positioning Los Angeles as a leading global destination for meetings and conventions. #MeetLA #LATourism #HBABC
