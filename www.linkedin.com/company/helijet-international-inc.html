About us

Helijet is proud to have been a part of the growth of British Columbia for the past three decades. In November 1986, armed with a vision, one helicopter and handful of dedicated employees Helijet began commuter service between downtown Vancouver and downtown Victoria. The first scheduled helicopter service in Canada! From that one helicopter and one route we have grown into a publicly traded company with a fleet of 10 helicopters, airplanes and over 100 employees. Helijet offers a wide range of scheduled, scenic and corporate charter services, serving Vancouver, Richmond, Victoria, Nanaimo, Haida Gwaii, Prince Rupert and spots inbetween. In addition Helijet is one of the largest Air Medical service providers in the Province. We look forward to helping the people of British Columbia get where they want to go for many decades to come.

    We are proud to celebrate the inclusion of Helijet’s President & CEO, Daniel Sitnam, in BIV News BC500 recognizing British Columbia's most influential business leaders. Since founding Helijet International Inc. in 1986, Daniel has guided the company from a single-helicopter operation to the world’s largest helicopter airline. Under his leadership, Helijet has grown into a diversified aviation company serving communities across British Columbia and beyond. With a team of more than 160 dedicated professionals, Daniel has fostered a culture of innovation, safety, and service excellence. A Canadian Aviation Hall of Fame (2017) inductee, Daniel has also received Lifetime Achievement Awards from both the Helicopter Association of Canada and the British Columbia Aviation Council (BCAC) as well as being recognized for outstanding contribution and leadership for the promotion and development of aviation in British Columbia. With thanks to BIV for recognizing his lasting contributions to the aviation industry and to British Columbia’s business community, we congratulate Daniel on this well-deserved honour and thank him for his continued leadership and vision.
