About us

The Entrepreneurs is a new television show and online series that aims to change how we see and value the women and men who drive our economy. Entrepreneurs are the engine of the economy. They're ingenious, relentless, and tireless innovators. Yet too often they are underappreciated, and at times vilified for succeeding. They are the forgotten heroes of our society, which is why we have chosen to lift up their voices with this program.
