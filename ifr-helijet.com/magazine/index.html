Helijet International Inc., is a Vancouver-based airline providing scheduled helicopter service between Victoria and Vancouver, and Vancouver and Nanaimo, as well as a wide range of charter services throughout British Columbia. The company is also one of the largest providers of air medical services in Western Canada.

For more information, visit the Helijet website.

Produced by Island Parent Group Enterprises Ltd., In-Flight Review (IFR) is an upscale lifestyle magazine tailored to meet the interests and expectations of Helijet’s unique clientele.
