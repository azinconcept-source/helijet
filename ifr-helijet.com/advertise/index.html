In-Flight Review (IFR) is an upscale lifestyle magazine tailored to meet the interests and expectations of Helijet’s unique clientele. The magazine is smart and fun with an emphasis on what’s new and original on the West Coast. Every issue includes informative and entertaining stories on the rich cultural trends of the Pacific Northwest, from the charm of Vancouver Island to the glittering excitement of Vancouver, and beyond.
